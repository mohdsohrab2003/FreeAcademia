You Will Find

Covering basic concepts and pragmatic skills, the "Cybersecurity Bootcamp" course offers a strong grounding in cybersecurity. Generally speaking, students should expect to learn the following:

  • Fundamentals of Cybersecurity:

    Know fundamental concepts about the need of cybersecurity in the present society and how it guards against numerous sorts of cyberattacks.

  • Types of Cyber Attacks:

    Learn how to identify and stop phishing, malware, ransomware, and DDoS attacks among various types of cyberattacks.

  • Security Frameworks and Protocols:

    Discover how SSL/TLS and other key cybersecurity systems help to safeguard data and communications.

  • Network Security:

    Research the principles of network security, that is, how to protect data in route via VPNs, intrusion detection systems, and firewalls.

  • Cybersecurity Tools:

    Using well-known cybersecurity tools and programs for system security, monitoring, and scanning, get pragmatic understanding.

  • Incident Response and Recovery:

    Learn how to manage security events including strategies for recovery, containment, and eradication.
